About Me

QA-QMS Specialist & Backend Developer

I am a dedicated Quality Management Specialist with hands-on experience in pharmaceutical quality systems, regulatory compliance, and digital documentation control. Over the years, I’ve been actively involved in managing CAPA systems, audit preparedness, regulatory inspections, and change management processes, ensuring that all operations align with GMP standards and global compliance expectations. My proficiency in tools like TrackWise, D2 Documentum, and MES platforms has enabled me to streamline workflows, enhance traceability, and support cross-functional teams effectively. I have successfully designed MES workflows, authored SOPs for critical manufacturing equipment, and led investigations that required a deep understanding of process deviation analysis and root cause identification. As an LMS Administrator, I’ve helped implement training programs and maintain regulatory training records, promoting a culture of continuous learning and compliance. Beyond my core profession, I am deeply passionate about Java Backend Development, with a focus on building scalable, secure, and efficient enterprise applications. My expertise spans across Spring Core, Spring MVC, Spring Boot, Spring Data JPA, Spring Security, and Spring Cloud. I’ve built and optimized RESTful APIs, implemented authentication and authorization using Spring Security, and handled data operations with PostgreSQL and MongoDB. I enjoy solving complex backend challenges through microservices architecture, event-driven design with Kafka, and containerized deployment using Docker and Kubernetes. My goal is to leverage my unique combination of domain knowledge in pharma and technical expertise in backend systems to bridge the gap between industry operations and smart software solutions. Whether it’s improving quality systems or architecting backend solutions, I thrive on solving real-world problems and continuously learning new technologies that push boundaries.
